How To


Welcome to SeeMore Interactive

Aim your smart phone or tablet at specially marked images to scan and see interactive content.

Hold camera still and avoid glare.

Image recognition can take a few seconds and may depend on your network speed. When interactive content is found it will immediately appear on your screen.

Products triggered on your device can be engaged by dragging them on the screen to the desired location.

Drag items to the main position to see details. Tap image will enable additional content if available.

Drag to cart enables e-commerce function.

Drag to share enables posting to social network.

Drag to Digital closet enables user to save items, create unique wish lists and share lists with friends.

Back arrow returns to previous screen. Hold back arrow to return to scanning screen.

Tap SeeMore Interactive logo to access menu.
